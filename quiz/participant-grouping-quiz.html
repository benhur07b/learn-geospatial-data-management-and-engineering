Who are you here to be?


Participant Self-Assessment Quiz • Complete individually • ~5 minutes

How to answer For each question, circle or tick the ONE answer that best describes you right now — not the person you want to become, but where you actually are today. There are no right or wrong answers. Your responses help us group participants so the hands-on exercises fit your experience level.

1 What is your primary responsibility related to geospatial data in your organization?

2 When a new dataset needs to be made available to the rest of your team or organization, who handles it?

3 Which of the following best describes something you have done in the past year?

4 When a published map layer or data service stops working (e.g. broken WMS, wrong projection, missing data), what happens?

5 Who is accountable for data quality — accuracy, completeness, and currency — in your organization?

6 How do you typically share geospatial data or analysis results with others?

7 How do you relate to access control and data permissions in your organization?

8 When a colleague cannot find a dataset they need for a project, what usually happens?

9 Which statement best describes how you think about geospatial data in your organization?

10 What would you most like to walk away with after this training?
